## 🧪 Research (Preliminary, not published yet...)

**"Identification of Common Profiles in Undergraduate Students Across Educational Systems at Tec de Monterrey"**
Random Forest + SHAP on 77,517 student records. Measured stability of dropout predictors across a major curriculum reform (Spearman ρ = 0.977). Finding: pre-reform models retain 98.6% discriminative power post-reform.

**"Effects of an Academic Resilience Workshop on Physiological Stress Responses in High School Students"**
Quasi-experimental design with EDA/PPG signals via ShimmerGSR+. Experimental group showed −101.3% sympathetic activation vs. +213.4% in control (p = 0.031).
